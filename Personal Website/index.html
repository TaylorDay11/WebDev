About Me

Hi, my name is Taylor.

I’m a recent college graduate with a passion for web development and user interface design. When I’m not glued to my laptop I like to craft, play video games, travel, and play with my dog. I also played on the CU Women's Rugby team for four years.

I have a unique degree, most people haven’t heard of TAM. It was an exciting program to be a part of because I was able to combine my technical skills with my creative skills. We were one of smallest majors on campus and had an amazing opportunity to work with a wide range of technology. Some of the coolest things I learned to do were build websites, program Arduino, 3D print, code apps, and design interfaces.
